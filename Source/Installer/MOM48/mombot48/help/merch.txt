                     
   
                 <<<< merch >>>>
   
                Visits all ports in grid and sells organics          
                and/or equipment.       
            
      merch {sector param} {min port product} [o | e] ({neg}otiate OR {hold}byhold)  
            {buyfuel} {docim}  
            
     Options:
         {neg/hold}   Determines planet negotiate or hold 
                      selling approach
          {skipcim}   Uses current cim data and skips searching
            {docim}   Does cim check before starting and skips searching
          {buyfuel}   Buys all the fuel in fuel selling ports 
                      on route  
             {half}   sell half of port (neg only for now)
