                     
   
                <<<< salesman >>>>
   
     Visits all ports in grid and buys fuel
     and sells/buys organics and equipment.
      
     salesman [min port product] ({neg}otiate OR {hold}byhold)
     {docim} {upgradefuel}
              
     Options: 
        {neg/hold}    Determines planet negotiate or hold selling
        {docim}       Does cim before starting route
        {upgradefuel} Upgrades fuel ports selling fuel
        {nohaggle}    Doesnt haggle when buying product
        {sellfuel}    Sells fuel during travels
            {grid}    Surround grid as you go
             {rob}    Rob ports after buying down
         {upgrade}    Slowly upgrade each port as it goes
