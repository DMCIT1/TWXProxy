                     
   
                  <<<< neg >>>>
   
                Planet Negotiate Trade Agrement          
                
         neg {f | o | e} {half}    
                                  
     Options:
              {f}   sells as much fuel as possible
              {o}   sells as much organics as possible
              {e}   sells as much equipment as possible
                                                         
           {half}   sell only half of port amount      
                                                         
               default is to sell all org and equip
