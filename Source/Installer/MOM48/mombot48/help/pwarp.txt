     pwarp {sector:#} {"trader_name"} 
           
             planet warps to sector 
           
         Options: 
                {sector:#} - sector to pwarp to 
           {"trader_name"} - trader to pwarp to
              
         Examples:
                    >p 233 - normal pwarp
              >p planet 12 - pwarp to last known 
                             location of planet 12 
                   >p mind - pwarp to a corp member with mind
                             in their name
          >p "mind dagger" - pwarp to corp member
